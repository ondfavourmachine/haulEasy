About Go Direct

GO Direct App Limited (Gas & Oil Direct) is a tech-enhanced service provider (Online Platform) founded by Travis Commodity Trading Company Limited and Innovate One Company Limited.

GO Direct has a unique methodology and technology-driven business that is created to solve and take advantage of the gaps in the market space, by redefining the diesel and gas (LPG) direct delivery business and services.

Designed and developed to be a solution to the need for home and corporate delivery of Diesel and LPG, by connecting buyers to sellers in-app. Where the transaction process is facilitated, transparent and simplified from order entry to delivery.

An aggregator that searches, profiles and analyzes price trends and market conditions in order to offer all customers the best prices, quality guaranteed products, efficient delivery services and customer focus support & feedback management services.
